# Decision D-028 - Trust Model Is Separate From Delivery Completion

Frozen decision:
- the full trust model should live in its own document, not inside `DELIVERY-COMPLETION-DEFINITION.md`

What that means:
- delivery completion consumes only the delivery-boundary trust condition
- the broader trust concept is wider than completion
- scoring, ownership, aggregation, and governance belong in a separate trust model

Why this was accepted:
- keeps `DELIVERY-COMPLETION-DEFINITION.md` focused on completion truth
- prevents the trust concept from being cramped into delivery-only language
- leaves room for trust to become a wider system concept later
