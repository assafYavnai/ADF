# Decision D-034 - Trust Is Primarily Governance Owned

Frozen decision:
- trust should be handled primarily in the governance layer

What that means:
- the mechanism should stay lightweight
- trust should not become manual bureaucracy
- governance is the natural place to record, compare, and synthesize trust signals

Why this was accepted:
- trust needs to be operational, not only discussed after the fact
- governance already owns cross-cutting control and lifecycle truth
- this creates the best path to future automation
