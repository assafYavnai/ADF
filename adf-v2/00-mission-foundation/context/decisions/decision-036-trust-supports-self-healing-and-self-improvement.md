# Decision D-036 - Trust Supports Self-Healing And Self-Improvement

Frozen decision:
- the trust model should support future self-healing and self-improving behavior

What that means:
- in the near term, low trust may trigger reporting, recommendation, and corrective action
- in later stages, the same model should support automatic tightening of controls, self-healing, and self-improvement
- trust is not only about escalation; it is also about system learning

Why this was accepted:
- aligns the model with the long-term v2 direction
- makes trust useful for both governance and improvement
- helps detect deep system weaknesses before they become repeated executive burden
