# Decision D-032 - Upward Box Trust Is Not Self-Published

Frozen decision:
- a box does not self-publish its own trust level upward

What that means:
- the parent holds trust in the child
- the child may separately hold trust in the parent
- upward trust reporting must not become self-certification

Why this was accepted:
- keeps upward trust honest
- avoids self-reported health claims
- preserves the bidirectional nature of trust while keeping reporting disciplined
