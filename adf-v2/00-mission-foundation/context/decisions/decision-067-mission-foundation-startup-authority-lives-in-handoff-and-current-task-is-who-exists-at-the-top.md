# Decision D-067 - Mission-foundation startup authority lives in HANDOFF.md and the current task is who exists at the top

Frozen decision:
- for mission-foundation startup, `context/HANDOFF.md` is the canonical restart authority
- `context/NEXT-STEP-HANDOFF.md` is only a thin checkpoint companion and must stay aligned to `HANDOFF.md`
- the restart frame should stay explicit as broader work, current task, next step, and later steps/open items
- the current task after `BOXED-COMPONENT-MODEL.md` is to define who exists at the top
- the exact filename and final shape of that thin top-level governing-entity document remain open and must not replace the real current task in startup answers

Why:
- this removes the startup split where the narrow checkpoint file overrode the broader restart frame
- it keeps fresh agents aligned to the real phase-`00` work instead of collapsing too early into document-naming discussion
- it parks filename choice as future detail while preserving the correct current-task abstraction
