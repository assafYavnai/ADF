# Decision D-017 - Complete Is Returned Artifact With No Leaked Burden

Frozen decision:
- `complete` is defined primarily by successful return of the requested artifact into the production tree, without leaked operational burden

What that means:
- completion is not defined primarily by internal process success alone
- internal process matters because it makes the returned result trustworthy
- the CEO-facing meaning of completion is that the CTO can truthfully certify the requested thing has been returned as a truly complete artifact in the production tree
- completion must not depend on leftover operational work being pushed past CTO governance back to the CEO

Why this was accepted:
- keeps the definition at the service boundary
- aligns completion with what the CEO actually buys from the system and what the CTO is accountable for certifying
- prevents internal route success from being mistaken for real delivery completion
