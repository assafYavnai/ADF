# Decision D-030 - Trust Is Bidirectional

Frozen decision:
- trust is bidirectional across adjacent boxes

What that means:
- each side may hold trust in the other side
- trust is not only top-down
- both sides of the relationship matter for diagnosis

Examples:
- CTO trust in Dev
- Dev trust in CTO
- Review trust in Implementor
- Implementor trust in Review

Why this was accepted:
- avoids one-sided blame models
- preserves both sides of the story
- helps expose whether failure comes from execution, review, handoff, or contract weakness
