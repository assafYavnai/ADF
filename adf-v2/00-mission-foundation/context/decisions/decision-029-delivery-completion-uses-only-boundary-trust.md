# Decision D-029 - Delivery Completion Uses Only Boundary Trust

Frozen decision:
- `DELIVERY-COMPLETION-DEFINITION.md` should use only the narrow delivery-boundary meaning of trust

What that means:
- the CEO can rely on the CTO completion claim without manual supervision, verification, reconstruction, or repair
- internal problems may happen, but they must be contained before completion is declared upward
- broader trust scoring and governance mechanics are out of scope for the completion document

Why this was accepted:
- keeps the current task bounded
- prevents wider trust-model questions from blocking completion definition
- makes clear which part of trust belongs to delivery completion now
