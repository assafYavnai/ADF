# Decision D-031 - Trust Is Edge-Based In A Workflow Graph

Frozen decision:
- trust is fundamentally edge-based inside a workflow graph

What that means:
- trust is held across adjacent relationships
- workflows are not purely sequential
- one node may sit on several trust edges at once

Why this was accepted:
- reflects the real shape of role and workflow interactions
- prevents trust from being modeled as one flat score per actor
- supports more accurate diagnosis of where reliability is weakening
