# Decision D-018 - Leaked Operational Burden Definition

Frozen decision:
- `leaked operational burden` should be defined at a high level as any work that should have been absorbed inside CTO governance and therefore must not be pushed up to the CEO in order to make the delivery truly complete after handoff

What that means:
- the document defines the boundary first at principle level
- the definition does not start as an implementation checklist
- concrete burden categories can still be named afterward where needed

Examples of the burden class:
- manual route supervision
- manual cleanup
- manual state or status repair
- manual resume driving
- manual environment hygiene repair

Why this was accepted:
- keeps the document high level but useful
- preserves the service-boundary meaning of completion
- gives a principled way to carry v1 pain into the delivery-definition contract without flattening the CTO layer out of the model
