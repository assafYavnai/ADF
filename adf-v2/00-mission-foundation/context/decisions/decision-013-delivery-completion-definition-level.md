# Decision D-013 - Delivery Completion Definition Level

Frozen decision:
- `DELIVERY-COMPLETION-DEFINITION.md` stays high-level but concrete

What that means:
- define the truth conditions for calling delivery complete
- define the truth conditions for calling delivery production-ready
- define what must not remain afterward
- do not turn the document into a workflow spec, implementation checklist, or component contract

Why this was accepted:
- keeps the document useful at CEO/CTO decision level
- prevents early overload
- gives later documents a stable target without collapsing into detailed implementation guidance
