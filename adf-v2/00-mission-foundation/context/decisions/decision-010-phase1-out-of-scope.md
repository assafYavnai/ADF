# Decision D-010 — Phase 1 out of scope

Frozen wording for `MISSION-STATEMENT.md`:

## Phase 1 out of scope
- full virtual company modeling
- later-company roles, departments, and business functions
- enterprise process breadth beyond the startup model
- rebuilding legacy ADF behavior for compatibility
- wide feature expansion before the startup model is defined and proven

Why this wording was accepted:
- keeps Phase 1 focused on the startup model rather than later-company breadth
- avoids dragging legacy ADF compatibility into the new source-of-truth effort
- prevents feature sprawl before the boxed model is defined and proven
