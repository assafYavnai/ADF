# Decision D-019 - Trust Is The Umbrella Burden Rule

Frozen decision:
- anything that breaks justified CEO trust in the CTO-governed delivery chain counts as leaked operational burden

What that means:
- trust is the umbrella principle
- leaked operational burden is not limited to visible repair work
- CEO-side doubt that requires checking, supervising, verifying, repairing, or cleaning the route is itself part of the burden

Examples under this rule:
- needing to check whether the route really worked
- needing to verify whether the output is truly complete
- needing to confirm whether hidden cleanup or state repair is still required
- needing to doubt whether parallel work polluted the working environment
- needing to manually review, verify, resume, or repair the route after handoff
- discovering that completion was declared before basic delivery conditions were actually true

Why this was accepted:
- keeps the definition tied to the real v1 pain
- prevents narrow burden lists from missing important trust failures
- makes the CEO-facing service promise about trustworthy transfer of responsibility, not only about visible cleanup
