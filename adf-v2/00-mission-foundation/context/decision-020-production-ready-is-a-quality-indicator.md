# Decision D-020 - Production-Ready Is A Quality Indicator

Frozen decision:
- `production-ready` is a quality indicator

What that means:
- the returned artifact can be used in production
- it is robust at a high level
- it is tested
- it is rigorous enough to do its job reliably
- it is expected to handle errors, edge cases, and normal operational reality at an appropriate level

What it does not mean:
- not merely that the functionality exists
- not merely that the route completed cleanly

Why this was accepted:
- keeps production-readiness tied to the quality of the returned artifact
- prevents clean closeout from being mistaken for real production fitness
- aligns the service promise with what the CEO expects when something is returned as complete and production-ready
