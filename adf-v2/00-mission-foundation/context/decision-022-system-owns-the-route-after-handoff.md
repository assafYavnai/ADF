# Decision D-022 - System Owns The Route After Handoff

Frozen decision:
- after handoff, the system owns the route until a truthful terminal result is reached

What that means:
- responsibility transfers at the service boundary
- the CEO does not operate the route after handoff
- the CTO owns the shaped handoff and truthful upward certification
- the governed delivery system remains responsible for execution until the work reaches a truthful terminal result

Why this was accepted:
- this is the base trust condition of the service
- it defines the transfer-of-responsibility promise at the center of the CEO -> CTO -> startup development team model
- other trust conditions such as queryability, resumability, cleanliness, and exception signaling support this rule
